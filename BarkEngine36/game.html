What does this do?

The Genral Processing Method determines how progects (In this case MTT) handels genral computing (Things that are not grathics related for example)! BPM 500 (Barkengine Processing Method) is the old, but proven system for this, whearas BPM 1000 is newer, and in some cases faster*! but does come with some rare instablitys.


If you want to know more specific information on the BPM 500/1000, here you go :D:

The BPM 500 (standing for "Barkengine Processing Method"), is the old, original method for general processing (This is mainly general computing tasks, asside from rendering witch is handeled by the Grathics Method!).

The BPM 500 has 1GB (Gigabyte) of Simulated Memory, and the BPM 1000 has about 2.3GB's of Simulated Memory!
